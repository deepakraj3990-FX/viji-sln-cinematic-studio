FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for weddings and major events. For smaller events like birthdays or baby showers, 2-4 weeks notice is usually sufficient.

What is your pricing structure?

+

Our pricing varies based on the type of event, duration, and deliverables required. We offer customizable packages to fit different budgets. Contact us for a personalized quote.

How long does it take to receive the photos?

+

We deliver edited photos within 2-4 weeks for most events. Wedding albums may take 4-6 weeks due to extensive editing and album design work.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

+

Yes! We love destination weddings and are available to travel anywhere in India and internationally. Travel and accommodation costs are quoted separately.

What equipment do you use?

+

We use professional-grade Canon and Sony cameras with a range of prime and zoom lenses. For videos, we use cinema cameras, gimbals, and drones for aerial shots.

Do you provide raw/unedited photos?

+

We typically deliver fully edited photos only, as they represent our best work. However, this can be discussed during the consultation based on your specific needs.
